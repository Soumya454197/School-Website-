About JPHS

JPHS is dedicated to providing high-quality education to nurture students' academic and personal growth. Our modern curriculum and state-of-the-art facilities help students unlock their full potential.

Academics

We offer a diverse range of subjects, including Science, Mathematics, Literature, and the Arts, alongside a comprehensive sports program. At JPHS, our academic curriculum is designed to foster critical thinking, creativity, and problem-solving skills.

Science & Technology

Our Science and Technology program gives students hands-on experience with modern laboratory equipment and real-world applications of science. We regularly host Science Fairs, Hackathons, and Robotics Competitions to encourage innovation.

Arts & Literature

The Arts and Literature program nurtures creativity. Students are encouraged to explore visual arts, creative writing, and theater. Annual Art Exhibitions and Poetry Slams are some of the highlights of this program.

Sports & Physical Education

We believe in the holistic development of our students, which is why we emphasize physical fitness through sports like football, basketball, cricket, and swimming. We have won several inter-school sports championships over the years.

Events

At JPHS, we believe that learning extends beyond the classroom. Our events program is designed to give students enriching experiences throughout the year.

Annual Cultural Fest

Our Cultural Fest is a grand celebration of diversity, where students showcase their talents in music, dance, and theater. It’s a week-long event filled with performances, art exhibitions, and cultural workshops.

Science Fair

The JPHS Science Fair is a platform for students to demonstrate their innovative science projects. Our young scientists present research, models, and experiments to the school community and external experts.

Sports Day

Sports Day is one of the most anticipated events at JPHS. Students participate in a range of athletic activities including track events, football matches, and relay races. It’s a day of teamwork, sportsmanship, and school spirit.

Debate Competitions

We host inter-school debate competitions that allow students to develop their public speaking and critical thinking skills. Our debating teams have won several regional and national-level competitions.

Alumni

Our alumni community consists of individuals who have gone on to achieve great things in their respective fields. From business leaders to social activists, JPHS alumni are making their mark worldwide.

Dr. Neha Sharma

Dr. Sharma is a renowned scientist currently working at NASA. She credits JPHS for instilling in her a passion for space exploration and science. She has been a keynote speaker at various international conferences.

Rohit Mehta

Rohit Mehta, a JPHS alumnus, is the founder and CEO of a successful tech startup in Silicon Valley. He often returns to JPHS to mentor students interested in entrepreneurship and technology.

Sneha Gupta

Sneha is a social activist working with the United Nations on education reforms in underprivileged areas. Her experiences at JPHS fueled her drive to make a difference in the world.
